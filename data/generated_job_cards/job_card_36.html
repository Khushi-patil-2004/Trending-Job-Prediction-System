About the Role The Business Technology Go-To-Market QA team is looking for a dedicated and highly motivated Quality Assurance (QA) Automation Engineer to execute test automation strategy and roadmap, develop key automation events, and provide quality and technical thought leadership in implementing automation for our Salesforce ecosystem and Adobe Experience Manager application using Tricentis tools. The team is on a mission to improve automation coverage aimed at improving QA efficiency and ensuring high-quality releases of Go-To-Market applications. The QA Automation Engineer will collaborate closely with Functional test engineers, Dev engineers, and BSAs. This is a individual contributor role and the individual is expected to be technical. We are seeking a QA Automation Engineer with Tosca automation tool, who possesses a strong understanding of automation best practices. If you are passionate about software quality and have experience in providing technical leadership to teams in test automation projects, we want to hear from you! About You Devise test automation strategy, develop the automation roadmap for Salesforce platform, aligning with the business requirements. Design and develop robust test automation suites using Tricentis Tosca automation tool and qTest tool. Develop automated test cases using Tosca, RTBs (Reusable Test Blocks). Implement best practices into the automation so that the automaton is highly stable, discovers defects, requires low maintenance and runs swiftly and selctively. Conduct technical reviews, provide guidance and be the technical point of contact to other team members on automation-related aspects and troubleshooting issues. Collaborate closely with development, business systems analysts to achieve comprehensive test coverage. Integrate automation suitess into the DevSecOps CI/CD pipelines collaborating with the DevOps team. Contribute to the growth and continuous improvement of quality and testing processes within the organization. Basic Qualifications 5+ years of experience as QA engineer for Web, APIs and CRM apps. 5+ years of experience in functional, E2E test automation using Tricentis Tosca for Salesforce platform. Proficiency in developing Tosca RTBs, TDM, TQL, DEX, scheduling test runs and generation of test results reports. Certification as Tosca Test automation architect (TA1) . Proficiency with Salesforce platform (Marketing, Sales, CPQ, Support, Services etc..) 3+ years of experience with test cases management tools such as qTest, TestRail. Proficiency in Object-Oriented Programming and in C# or Java/Python 2+ years of DevSecOps experience, integrating automated tests into CI/CD Pipelines. Other Qualifications Experience testing plans test case design techniques, test execution, UAT and regression. Experience with OAuth, Single-Sign-On, JSON and XML. Experience in testing REST Web Services and Postman. Experience with AWS cloud and its services EC2, S3, RDS. Familiarity with relational databases (Oracle, SQLServer). Experience with Git for version control. Experience in creating automated tests using tools such as Selenium Web Driver, Page Object Model and Cucumber.
